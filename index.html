Pietro Capovilla

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at Scuola Normale Superiore, in Pisa, under the supervision of Roberto Frigerio.

I study geometry and topology. My favorite topological invariants are bounded cohomology and simplicial volume.

I am one of the organizers of the Baby Geometri Seminars in Pisa.
