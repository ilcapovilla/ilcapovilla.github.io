Pietro Capovilla

I am a postdoc researcher in Mathematics at the University of Cyprus, in Nicosia, in the research group of Christoforos Neofytidis. Before that, I was a PhD student at Scuola Normale Superiore, in Pisa, under the supervision of Roberto Frigerio.

I study geometry and topology.
